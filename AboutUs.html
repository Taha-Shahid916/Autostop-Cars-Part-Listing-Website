About Us

Founded in 2021, AutoStop has become Pakistan's most renowned automotive portal. Since its birth, AutoStop has helped millions of Pakistanis buy and sell automobiles, check automotive prices, and find solutions to their automotive needs. Today, AutoStop are the first name that comes to mind for anyone looking to purchase their ride.

AutoStop has been busy serving its customers. Since its launch, we have achieved a milestone of 1 million happy customers by buying and selling over 400,000 vehicles.

This kind of success has made AutoStop a prominent name in the global e-commerce and classifieds sectors and helped raise $2 million in the first round of funding.

AutoStop is a hub where buyers and sellers interact and is a comprehensive automotive portal with a forum dedicated to all automotive discussions.

At AutoStop, we provide our visitors with the best online experience. Our mission is to revolutionize and continuously add value to the way people buy and sell vehicles online in Pakistan. We aim to provide our users with the most comprehensive automotive knowledge concerning Pakistan and the world alike and help them develop a sense of belonging in the automotive community.
